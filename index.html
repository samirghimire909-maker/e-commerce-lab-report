Good to know

Is this a real store?

No — CircuitCraft is a sandbox e-commerce build for a digital marketing & analytics lab. Payments are simulated and no data is transmitted anywhere.

Can I connect GA4?

Yes — drop your Measurement ID into script.js where marked, and this site will report pageviews, add_to_cart, and purchase events.
